My opinions on Naraka

  • Vincent DEVINE
  • 08/08/2023
What is Naraka ?

Naraka: Bladepoint is a battle royale video game developed by the Chinese studio 24 Entertainment.
Immersing players in a world inspired by Asian mythology, the game stands out with its smooth and vertical gameplay, offering quick and intense battles.
Players fight for supremacy using melee weapons, bows, and unique skills. The interactive environment allows players to climb, slide, and move skillfully within the game's open world.
Naraka: Bladepoint provides a refreshing experience in the battle royale genre with its blend of fast-paced action and innovative mechanics.

My opinions

I personally find Naraka interesting. Its movement dynamics are reminiscent of Apex, and the varied heroes offer distinct styles. From my experience, the player's skills seem to have a greater impact than the chosen hero. The battles, while complex, remain intuitive due to various weapon-specific combos. Despite the limited number of weapons, after 30 hours of gameplay, I haven't exhausted the interest in a specific weapon yet.
Since the game has transitioned to free-to-play, I recommend trying it out to experience a different kind of battle royale (due to its melee-focused combat).
