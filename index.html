About me

I've always been intrigued by the inner workings and underlying logic of everyday things. It's with this mindset and the desire to learn more that I delved into programming, aiming to better comprehend the tool I use on a daily basis.

Video game programming came later, during a school project or after observing my classmates play the games we had just created. Witnessing the joy, sadness, and excitement it brought forth deeply impacted me. It's this array of emotions that inspired me to bring all these sensations to a wider audience, all while doing what I love.

Apart from programming, I have a strong interest in many other activities and I continuously strive to explore new experiences. However, the areas that captivate me the most are video games (FPS, RPG, and TPS), music (Rap, Pop, Metal, Electro), ancient Egypt (architecture, mythology), and reading (manga, comics, science fiction).
