Contexte

For ISART DIGITAL, 1st year project.
First inter-year project aimed at teaching teamwork and the importance of communication. The constraint of this project is to design a 2.5D game from scratch.

Description

Challenge your friends in thrilling races for up to 4 players. Change lanes to avoid obstacles, take shortcuts, and tackle your friends to gain an advantage.
A 2.5D racing game for PC, created with Unity by a team of 10 (3 developers, 6 game designers, and 1 music and sound designer).
